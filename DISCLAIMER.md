# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides German farm subsidy data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional agricultural, financial, or legal advice. Subsidy scheme details, payment rates, and eligibility criteria are based on published German government sources (BMEL, BLE, Laender), but schemes change and individual circumstances vary. Always check the latest information from your Bundesland's Zahlstelle and the BMEL GAP-Strategieplan before making applications or land management decisions.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **GAP-Strategieplan Deutschland 2023-2027** -- BMEL (Bundesministerium fuer Ernaehrung und Landwirtschaft)
- **BLE Foerderinformationen** -- Bundesanstalt fuer Landwirtschaft und Ernaehrung
- **GAK-Rahmenplan** -- Gemeinschaftsaufgabe Agrarstruktur und Kuestenschutz
- **Konditionalitaet (GLOZ/GAB)** -- BMEL / BLE / Laender-Kontrollbehoerden
- **Laender-Agrarprogramme** -- FAKT (BW), KULAP (BY), HALM (HE), NiB-AUM (NI) and others

All data is from official government publications (amtliche Veroeffentlichungen).

## Currency

Subsidy schemes change. Payment rates, eligibility criteria, and application windows are updated by BMEL, BLE, and the Laender annually. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
